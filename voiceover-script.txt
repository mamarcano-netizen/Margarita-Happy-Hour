HAPPY HOUR WITH MARGARITA
Full Glam Tutorial — Voiceover Script
For use with ElevenLabs Speech Synthesis

PASTE THIS ENTIRE BLOCK INTO ELEVENLABS AS ONE GENERATION:

----------------------------------------------------------------

Hey gorgeous — welcome back to Happy Hour with Margarita.
I am so excited you're here because tonight... we are doing FULL glam.
No filter, no shortcuts — just me, my brushes, and everything I know.
So grab your makeup bag, get comfortable, and let's get into it.

[pause]

First things first — skin prep.
And I cannot stress this enough — your makeup is only as good as the skin underneath it.
I always start with a hydrating primer all over the face.
This is what gives you that gorgeous lit-from-within glow that makes people stop and ask — girl, what are you wearing?
Take your time here. Press it in gently. Let it absorb.
Your foundation will thank you.

[pause]

Now — foundation.
I am using a full coverage formula today because we are going ALL the way in.
The secret is in the application — I use a damp beauty sponge and I press and bounce, press and bounce.
Never drag. Dragging lifts the primer and leaves streaks.
We don't want that.
Build it where you need it — a little more around the nose, the chin, anywhere that needs extra coverage.

[pause]

Concealer time.
Under the eyes — go two shades lighter than your foundation.
This brightens the whole face and makes you look awake even when you are not.
And then right down the center of the nose, the forehead, the chin — that is your highlight map.
Set everything with a translucent powder so it does not move.
Not even a little.

[pause]

Now THIS is where the magic happens — contour and bronze.
I am taking a cool toned contour shade right underneath the cheekbones.
Think — suck in your cheeks, feel that hollow? That is where the contour goes.
Along the jawline, sides of the forehead, tip of the nose — blend everything out beautifully.
Then warm the whole face back up with a bronzer on the cheeks and across the nose.
This is how you get that snatched but natural finish.

[pause]

Blush. Do not skip the blush.
I see so many people skip blush and wonder why their makeup looks flat.
A soft rose or peach blush right on the apples of the cheeks and blended up toward the temples —
it gives life. It gives warmth. It gives EVERYTHING.

[pause]

Now let's talk about these eyes.
This is the star of the whole look tonight.
I started with a matte transition shade in the crease — something warm and blended.
This is what creates depth before we even add any drama.
Then I packed a deep brown almost black shadow right into the outer corner and worked it inward in small circular motions.
The number one rule of a smokey eye — build it in layers.
Do not put all the dark color on at once. Layer it, blend it, check it, add more if needed.

[pause]

Eyeliner — I tightlined my upper waterline with a black kohl pencil.
This makes your lashes look thicker and fuller without that heavy liner look.
Then a thin wing on the upper lid — just a small flick, nothing too dramatic because the eyeshadow is already doing the heavy lifting.

[pause]

Lashes.
I applied my falsies as close to the lash line as possible — let the glue get tacky first, always.
Then I sealed everything with two coats of mascara, top and bottom,
and smudged a little dark shadow right underneath the lower lash line for that sultry, smokey finish.
Look at those eyes.
Look at them.

[pause]

And now — the lip.
My absolute favorite part.
Tonight I went with a deep wine berry shade because it is dramatic, it is bold, and it is absolutely gorgeous.
I lined slightly outside my natural lip line to add fullness —
a little trick that makes all the difference.
Fill it in, blot, apply one more layer, blot again.
That is how you get a lip that lasts all night.

[pause]

Last step — setting spray.
Hold it about twelve inches from your face,
close your eyes,
and mist it all over in a figure eight motion.
This locks everything in place and gives the skin that beautiful second-skin finish
that makes people think you woke up like this.

[pause]

And that — is the full Happy Hour glam look.

From skin prep to setting spray — this is the look that gets you noticed,
gets you complimented,
and most importantly makes you feel like the most confident, most beautiful version of yourself.
Because that is what Happy Hour with Margarita is all about.

If you recreate this look I want to see it —
tag me, send me a message, show me what you created.
I am so proud of every single one of you for showing up for yourselves.

New content drops every single week right here.
So if you have not already — subscribe, follow, and join the Happy Hour Club.
The link is in my bio and at Happy Hour With Margarita dot com.

Until next time gorgeous —
your happy hour... starts now.
